Short History

Noah’s Orchards is a small boutique distilling company that produces Armenian premium quality fruit brandies. Noah’s Orchards distilling company is a family business that focuses not on the quantity, but quality. Joined in rich tradition and craftsmanship of finest brandy production, quality of handpicked fruits and latest distillation technology, Noah’s Orchards distilling company brings a premium fruit brandy ARALEZ made entirely from fruits for your every occasion.

Brandy

Whether you sip it in a snifter or mix it into cocktails, brandy is a great distilled spirit to have in your bar. It's produced around the world as Cognac, Armagnac, pisco, grappa, schnapps and eau-de-vie, from grapes or other types of fruit. Brandy was also one of the most popular spirits used in the first bars, which is why it dominates lists of the best classic cocktails.

Aged Brandy

Our aged brandies are the pure essence of the fruit, lightly aged in oak for a warm vanilla tinge to the beautifully captured quality of the fresh farm fruit. Think of them as the equivalent of Grappa or Calvados, but made from local fruit. So they are not “fruit-flavored brandies”, which are usually made using a base grape spirit, then flavored with extracts and artificial flavorings. Aralez aged brandies are the perfect capture of the wholesome, natural fruit in each bottle.
