Discovering the Aralez

Aralez is an Armenian premium class fruit brandy fully handmade and handcrafted. Every single drop of Aralez spirits are made from the finest fruits, using meticulous processes. Each bottle of Aralez Brandy is a striking combination of quality, craftsmanship and taste. We use premium German pot steels for a perfect distillation. Many of the world’s most innovative distillers choose Carl distillery equipment as we do.

Our spirits are kept in special cellars and are carefully monitored under 24-hour non stop classical or spiritual music. Our barrels are completely handcrafted with oak wood from the Limousin forest in France. Our branding and packaging design is made by a world famous multi award winning Spanish company SeriesNemo. The only 2 things we use Armenian are our fantastic water and incomparable fruits. All our production has limited quantity and we guarantee unique taste and excellent quality for each of them.

History of the Roots

ARALEZ is a premium quality brandy derived by classic double distillation from 100% fruit base.

We do not use any sugar, aromas, colors or any artificial food additive to our production.

Aralez Brandy is produced from selected sorts of fruit prepared especially for brandy production which contain the right amount of sugar needed to start the process of obtaining perfect levels of alcohol through alcoholic fermentation before the distillation process. The traditional craft of Aralez makers taught us that best fruit brandy contains in itself all sensory-specific satiety of the fruit from which is being derived from.

Unique in its versatility, fruit brandy has been present as a traditional and everyday drink of many nations. It is known to be consumed in all occasions, before, during or after the meal, during all day and it has special celebratory value in many cultures.

Rich Legacy of Aralez

Technology for distillation process has changed significantly through history as technology advanced and today we can produce product of highest quality in a completely controlled environment. Technological process of making fruit brandy itself has been same for hundreds of years only to preserve the fruity richness of taste and traditional feel.

Production process of fruit brandies consists of following:

  • Preparation of fruits
    Cleaning, destoning, grinding and pulping of fruits to speed up the fermentation process.
  • Preparing for fermentation
    Adding water, yeast, controlling the pH value of the fermentation liquid.
  • Distillation
    Technological process of extracting alcohol from the fermentation liquid by evaporation and condensation. This is the most sensitive process as ratio of chemical compounds directly affects the quality of the brandy. After the second distillation and reaching right alcohol levels, the fruit brandy spirit is finished.
  • Maturation, Aging process
    Fruit brandy is left to mature in oak barrels in a constant temperature between 15°C - 20°C. In this process fruit brandy develops its rich aroma and color. Usually we keep our spirits in oak barrels for 5 years in order to keep natural taste and aromas of fruit.
  • Finishing
    After maturation, we add one more process before our brandy is completely done. We call it finishing. We use wet wine oak casks Marsala (Sicily) for finishing process of apple brandy (Calvados style) and wet wine oak casks Banyuls (France) for finishing process of grape brandy (Grappa style).

End result of distillation is fruit brandy, clear or aged, which depends on type of fruit used and the way in which it was contained. Advantage of fruit brandy compared to other brandies is in its unprecedented fullness of taste and its aroma which can be directly related to the quality of fruits and traditional distillation process. This results in a beverage of high quality, recognizable taste and unique experience.

INSPIRING ARMENIAN GARDENS

Unique Taste of Fruites

We would like to present you Armenian fruits, which you should definitely try while visiting Armenia. The interrogation of consumers executed by us shows that the fruits grown in Armenia considerably differ in a positive side to taste, aroma and an external trade look from the same fruits grown up in other countries.

According to scientific researches, such good characteristics of fruits are caused first of all by the geographical location of Armenia, its natural-climatic conditions, abundance of sunlight, presence of fresh mountain spring waters and other important factors which create almost perfect conditions for cultivation of apricots, pears, peaches, apples, black and red plums, grapes and other kinds of fruits. Yeah, it’s out of any suspicion, Armenia is the country where the tastiest and juiciest fruits are grown.

WATER IN ARMENIA

One of the Most Delicious and Cleanest in the World

Water of Armenia is known to be one of the tastiest, most delicious and purest in the whole world without any exaggerations.

The water is directly supplied from the pure and natural sources. One can drink it straight from the tap. No need to boil and have any fears for your health! Water for Armenians is very special and they deeply honor it by composing songs, making stories and legends. They really appreciate this gift of nature. Water for Armenians symbolizes life. You can hear Armenians say 'May your life be as long as water' very often. This is a way of showing gratitude and is actually a blessing.

The water in Armenia is something of a miracle and is rumored to be the tastiest, sweetest drinking water in the world.
